AFROTAME 200A


Afrotame is an A grade artificial sweetener made by Aphrodite Foods.

It is a food additive that is around 200x times sweeter than sugar. Only trace amounts of Afrotame is required to sweeten food.  It is produced from starch by partial hydrolysis and is usually found as a white hygroscopic spray-dried powder. We use the best and most updated machinery to finely cut and blend each particle of Afrotame.

Afrotame is moderately heat-stable, effective even in small amounts, easily digestible, can be absorbed as rapidly as glucose and does not appear to accumulate in the body. It may be either moderately sweet or almost flavourless, depending on the amount used.

It is commonly used for the production of beverages, syrups, baked goods, chewing gum, dairy products, frozen dessert, yogurt, confectionery and other processed foods.
